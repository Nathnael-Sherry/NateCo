At NateCo, we are dedicated to providing exceptional products and services to our valued customers. We understand that you may have questions about our offerings, policies, and procedures. This resource is designed to address common inquiries and provide clarity on various aspects of our business. We are committed to transparency and open communication, and we hope that this FAQs section will serve as a valuable tool in addressing your queries. If you cannot find the information you are looking for, please do not hesitate to reach out to our customer support team, who will be more than happy to assist you. Thank you for choosing NateCo.   • 1. Will my business website be optimized for different sizes?

    Yes, all of our website designs and development of websites will be optimized for the five standard screen sizes (large desktop, laptop, tablet, phablet, and mobile phone). Over 50% of website traffic comes from mobile devices so we put a huge emphasis on making sure that your website performs on every single screen that someone might be viewing your site on.

  • 2. Do I get a say in the creative design of my business website?

    Of course! All of our projects start with a design phase that is based on a website questionnaire that takes into account your likes and tastes. The other great part? We design until we get it right, which means no revision limitations like most other companies place on their website designs.

  • 3. Do I own my business website once it is completed?

    Yes, yes, and yes. You own everything we design and develop. Don’t trust web developers that make you pay a monthly fee and then threaten to turn off your website if you stop paying.

  • 4. How quickly will I see SEO results?

    It depends and we offer NO guarantees. Anyone that does offer you guarantees might as well be a snake-oil salesman. SEO takes time, effort, and patience. When we first start, we normally target lower competitive keywords with average amounts of traffic since these are easier to rank for. As time goes on and we gain more ground on the lower competitive keywords we will start to target the more competitive keywords.
    Generally, ranking improvements can take anywhere from 3-6 months, with lasting effects generally coming in at around one year. The beauty is that SEO rankings, if maintained, will be extremely beneficial for you in the future and will create incredible ROI as time moves forward. The only wrong decision you can make in regards to SEO is doing nothing at all.
    SEO is like a snowball rolling downhill. At first it starts out small and slow, but as efforts increase and more and more SEO efforts are added, the snowball begins to get bigger and bigger and starts to roll down the hill out of control. The same will happen with your website traffic!

  • 5. Do I have to give you access to my social media accounts?

    Yes, we ask that you grant us access to your accounts in order for us to do our job correctly.
